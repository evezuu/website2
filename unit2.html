Primary and Secondary Succession

Primary succession: It occurs when there is no existing soil in the environment. The rocks take long time to become soil through weathering and erosion. Time: 500-1000+ years.

Secondary succession: It occurs with existing soil. It often occurs after major environmental catastrophes such as hurricanes, wildfires, or human-caused events such as mining and deforestation. Time: 100-500 years.
